Internships

Google Talent Sprint

The program is a combination of boot camps, live online classes, ongoing mentorship, certification and team-based projects to enhance problem-solving and computational thinking. It also provides exemplary participants with a chance to get career opportunities at high end product companies including Google.

Eligibility: Women Students currently in the first year of engineering pursuing CSE or equivalent streams

Application Period: January to February

Google STEP Internship

STEP is a 12-week internship for students with a passion for computer science. The internship program has a focus of providing development opportunities to students from groups historically underrepresented in tech, through technical training and professional development.

Eligibility: First and second-year undergraduate that have taken atleast 1-2 college level computer science courses.

Application Period: Different times of the year for different places. Starts from September tentatively

Microsoft Codess

Codess is a community for female coders initiated by Microsoft. It is established to explore ways to promote gender diversity in the engineering field. For Indians, Microsoft holds an online coding test in the month of February to invite women to their annual Codess event held in Hyderabad/Banglore. People invited to the event have a chance of getting recruited as a summer intern at Microsoft.

Eligibility: Second-year Indian female undergraduate students pursuing computer science or related fields, from selected institutes of the country.

Application Period: January to February

AmazeWiT - AmazeWOW by Amazon

Development initiative with internship and FTE SDE (Full-time Software Development Engineer) opportunities for Amazon in India, focused on bringing in top tech talent from engineering institutes. AmazeWoW aims at building a gender balance at the entry level for SDE role, so that with the Amazon's talent nurturing environment, the future leadership pipeline of Amazon technology team is more diverse.

Eligibility: Indian female undergraduate students pursuing computer science or related fields.
Second year students: For 2 month internship roles next year
Third year students: For 6 month internship/FTE roles next year

Application Period: April to May

MLH Fellowship

The MLH Fellowship aims to create a high-quality remote internship alternative that's available to students worldwide. It's designed from the ground up to recreate the best of aspects of a great internship experience with a focus on education, mentorship, and fun. Spend 12 weeks leveling up your software engineering skills by contributing to the Open Source software the world depends on.

Eligibility: Age 18+, Currently enrolled in a degree or diploma granting course of study; or have graduated from one within the previous 6 months, eligible to work in the country you will reside in during the program.

Application Period: April to May
