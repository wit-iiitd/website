Virtual Events

Exploring the Tech World

Hosts: Bhavya Chopra, Pragya Sikka, Ria Gupta, Shubhi Singhal, Vrinda Narayan

WiT conducted a session to provide an overview of opportunities in CS and related domains, to help students explore and navigate different opportunities such as open source, competitive programming, entrepreneurship, research, and much more, during their undergraduate studies.

Generation Google Scholarship AskMeAnything

Hosts: Vrinda Narayan, Ananya Jain, Larika Sehgal

WiT conducted a webinar, followed by an AMA session for students applying to the Generation Google Scholarship. Listen to past Women Techmakers scholars Vrinda Narayan, Larika Sehgal and Ananya Jain share tips and talk about the application process.

Pursuing an MBA in the US

Host: Aman Agarwal

Aman Agarwal graduated from IIITD in 2017 and went to NYU Stern School of Business to pursue an MBA where he was one of the Berkley Scholars. Currently, he is working as an associate at a-connect in the US. He takes us through his MBA journey, gives application tips and shares his experience studying in the US.

The Google STEP Journey

Hosts: Samiya Caur, Arundhati Bhattacharya

Women In Tech, IIITD is conducting a webinar followed by an AMA session on the Google STEP internship, hosted by former STEP interns, Samiya and Arundhati. The Google STEP internship is a wonderful opportunity for students to kick-start their careers in the field of technology.
